Yosemite National Park

This past summer I went to Yosemite National Park with friends and family. It was my first time. Though it was raining, we had a blast. Why? Because we're the type of people who can create their own fun. We crossed rivers fully clothed, hiked trails paving the way with horse dung, and a few celebs dawning the place seemingly incognito. But their rented jockey equipment didn't do the trick. Epic fail.
